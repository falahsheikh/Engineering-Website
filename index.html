About Me


Welcome to my profile! I'm Abdul Hady Shaik Hassan, a seasoned professional with a strong background in project execution and quality assurance. Passionate about power systems intricacies, I've played a pivotal role in shaping and maintaining critical infrastructures.


With a proven track record, I've overseen and executed power distribution and utility management projects, ensuring seamless integration of electrical systems in compliance with international standards.


Currently serving as Project Executor & QA/QC Inspector at MARAFIQ Power and Utility Company in Yanbu, KSA, I orchestrate Power Connection Requests, conduct inspections, and lead project execution in line with NEC, IEC, IEEE guidelines for diverse projects within the Royal Commission Yanbu.


During my extensive tenure at MARAFIQ, I excelled as QC & Quality Inspector/Electrical Foreman, evaluating technical aspects of distribution network projects and pioneering electrical distribution activities. I also played a key role in training and mentoring personnel.


From 1991 to 2002, as Substation Maintenance Electrical Foreman Gas Insulated Switch Gear (GIS) at the Royal Commission for Jubail and Yanbu, I undertook preventive and corrective maintenance of GIS, transformers, and circuit breakers, specializing in high-voltage testing, diagnostic maintenance, and troubleshooting.


In my earlier role as Electrical Commissioning Foreman from 1986 to 1991 at RESOURCE SCIENCES ARABIA LTD in Yanbu, I conducted commissioning tests on transformers and carried out preventive and corrective maintenance on various electrical systems.


Driven by a passion for excellence and a commitment to the highest standards, I bring a wealth of experience and expertise to any project. My journey is marked by achievements, recognition, and a relentless pursuit of knowledge in the dynamic field of electrical engineering. If you're seeking a professional with a proven track record and a dedication to quality, I'm ready to contribute to your endeavors.
